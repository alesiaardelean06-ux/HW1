Here are some of my all time favourite projects that I have been involved in during my time as a volunteer.

Projects
Project Name Type Description Results Social Impact
Christmas For Everyone Charity/Social A district-level project through which the club supported children from disadvantaged backgrounds by donating packages containing non-perishable food, books, clothes, shoes, and toys. Approximately 250 packages donated. Provided material support and brought joy during the holiday season, improving living conditions for children in need.
Ghiozdanul de Școală Educational/Charity Another districtual project, successfully completed, aimed at supporting children before the beginning of the school year. 55 children supported. Facilitated access to education, reduced financial difficulties, and brought happiness to children and their families.
Sprinkle It Fundraising Event/Charity A creative dessert-themed event that brought people together through teamwork and imagination, transforming sweet moments into meaningful support for those in need. Two centers for young people with special needs were supported with the decorated cupcakes. Encouraged community involvement and creativity.
Dunk It Sports/Charity An exciting basketball tournament that united young players while contributing to a greater cause. Children and teenagers enjoyed the event and contributed to the cause. Promoted sportsmanship, inclusion, teamwork, and community engagement while raising funds for charitable causes.
Sleeping Sun Cultural/Fundraiser An outdoor movie screening event organised to raise funds for children in need. Indirect beneficiaries: children supported through the Ghiozdanul de Școală project. Combined culture with civic engagement, encouraging the community to support children's education.
